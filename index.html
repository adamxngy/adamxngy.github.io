About

I'm a data analyst with a passion for uncovering insights and solving problems through data. With 5 years of experience in Microsoft Excel and proficiency in tools like Power BI and Tableau, I enjoy creating visually compelling dashboards and reports that tell a story. I am also skilled in Python, leveraging libraries such as Pandas and NumPy for data analysis, and have experience with MySQL for database management.

Currently, I am furthering my expertise by pursuing a part-time degree in Computer Science, deepening my technical knowledge and broadening my skill set. Problem-solving and analysis are more than just work for me—they're challenges I genuinely enjoy tackling. Whether it's uncovering trends or optimizing processes, I thrive on finding innovative solutions through data.
